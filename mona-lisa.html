Mona Lisa

Artist: Leonardo da Vinci

Year of Creation: 1503–1506 (completed around 1517)

Description:
Mona Lisa is one of the world's most famous paintings. It is renowned for the subject's mysterious smile, realistic details, and Leonardo da Vinci's masterful use of light and shadow. The painting is displayed in the Louvre Museum in Paris.
