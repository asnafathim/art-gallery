Girl with a Pearl Earring

Artist: Johannes Vermeer

Year of Creation: Around 1665

Description:
Girl with a Pearl Earring is one of Johannes Vermeer's most celebrated paintings. Often called the "Mona Lisa of the North," it is admired for the subject's expressive gaze, soft lighting, and the striking pearl earring.
