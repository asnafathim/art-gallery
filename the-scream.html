The Scream

Artist: Edvard Munch

Year of Creation: 1893

Description:
The Scream is one of the most iconic paintings in art history. It portrays a figure with an expression of fear and anxiety against a dramatic sky. The artwork symbolizes human emotion, loneliness, and existential anxiety.
