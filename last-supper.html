The Last Supper

Artist: Leonardo da Vinci

Year of Creation: 1495–1498

Description:
The Last Supper is one of Leonardo da Vinci's greatest masterpieces. It depicts the moment when Jesus Christ announces that one of his twelve disciples will betray him. The painting is admired for its realistic expressions, composition, and use of perspective.
