Starry Night

Artist: Vincent van Gogh

Year of Creation: 1889

Description:
The Starry Night is one of Vincent van Gogh's most famous paintings. It depicts a peaceful village beneath a dramatic, swirling night sky filled with bright stars and a glowing crescent moon. The painting is celebrated for its expressive brushstrokes and vibrant colors.
